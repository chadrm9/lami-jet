Enter your terms into the Search box and use the and buttons.

Results will contain linked descriptions and commodities. Selecting them will refine your search to those exact terms, as shown in the Search box.

Your orders are sorted by serviced date in descending order. Toggle the sort direction by using the button.

Filter:

Selecting a linked Chain & Store # will filter the orders list to show only matching titles.

Selecting a Sent link will filter the list to show only sent orders.

The button will remove all filters from the list.
